About Me

I’m currently a student at the Ada Developers Academy in Seattle, Washington. I graduated from The Evergreen state college with a Liberal Arts Degree. I’ve worked as a pastry chef, technical support engineer and a project manager.

I’m interested in becoming a full stack developer one day and make use of the problem solving skills I learned while supporting the work of other developers. In my spare time I love to bake and write short stories. I’m also passionate about sustainable practices and how we can improve the state of our environment.
